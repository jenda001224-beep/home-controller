After flashing
1
Device shows "Connecting to WiFi…" — if it hasn't connected before, a hotspot named SwitchPro-Setup starts automatically.
2
On your phone join the SwitchPro-Setup WiFi network. A portal opens — enter your home WiFi name & password and save.
3
Device connects to WiFi and shows its IP address on screen (e.g. http://192.168.1.42). Open that address in Safari.
4
Enter your DIRIGERA hub IP, tap Pair, then press the small button on the back of the DIRIGERA hub within 30 s.
5
Device reboots and shows your rooms and lights. Tap any tile to open the detail panel — brightness slider & colour wheel for lights. The physical button below the display goes back to the home screen.
↺
Factory reset: hold the BOOT button for 3 seconds on startup.
