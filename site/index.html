Home Controller

Apple Home-style controller for LilyGO T-Display S3 Pro.
Plug in your board, then click Install.

⚠️ Web Serial requires Chrome or Edge on desktop.
Safari and Firefox are not supported.

After flashing

1
Your display shows "HomeController-Setup"
2
Connect your phone to the HomeController-Setup WiFi network
3
A setup page opens. Enter your WiFi name & password and your Home Assistant IP + Long-Lived Token
4
Save → device reboots → your rooms appear. Tap to toggle, long-press for brightness & colour.

Requires Chrome or Edge on desktop  ·  Board pinout
